## Key Observations

- **Corporate Actions has been the highest-velocity domain** - 6+ projects shipped since 2023, including the NNX migration pieces
- **Securities Lending** had two major projects (international lending + automatic compensation)
- **Securities Borrowing** had one focused project (intraday shorting improvement)
- **SFTR 2.0** is proposed but not started - worth clarifying scope and priority for the team split

## Relevance to Team Split

When splitting Shark into CA team (4 devs) and SecFinance team (2 devs), this history shows:

- **CA team** inherits the most active development area with ongoing NNX migration
- **SecFinance team** inherits a more operationally focused area with the retail lending build ahead
- **SFTR 2.0** needs a team assignment decision during the split
